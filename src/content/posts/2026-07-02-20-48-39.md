## 直奔主题

## W-8表格签署

- 审核过程中可以把W-8先签了

![iShot_2026-01-08_14.53.06](https://cdn3.ldstatic.com/optimized/4X/a/3/8/a388c83d7f4531f8f33d68b8771cce21981c957b_2_464x499.jpeg)

- 选`立即认证`

![iShot_2026-01-08_14.53.24](https://cdn3.ldstatic.com/optimized/4X/d/d/d/dddea959e7080a0a1a18583ab639879313f99e3d_2_690x365.jpeg)

- 确认一遍个人信息

![iShot_2026-01-08_14.53.54](https://cdn3.ldstatic.com/optimized/4X/3/a/7/3a7458b468e7a9daf372f00efea30b8617535645_2_517x245.jpeg)

- 保存`确认码`

> 下面还需要你选一个安全问题，防止你手抖没有复制确认码
>
> ![image](https://cdn3.ldstatic.com/optimized/4X/f/f/a/ffa05eac059d8e9af42df2101949366db8755e41_2_345x109.png)

- 选择`W-8BEN`表

![iShot_2026-01-08_15.02.55](https://cdn3.ldstatic.com/optimized/4X/4/0/5/40597f534d0c3c743f4aae9ad7f39700b566ecf4_2_517x304.jpeg)

![iShot_2026-01-08_15.03.18](https://cdn3.ldstatic.com/optimized/4X/5/6/5/565c8407e1450d0c29654baaca6193cb6ab57a5f_2_412x375.jpeg)

![iShot_2026-01-08_15.04.08](https://cdn3.ldstatic.com/optimized/4X/0/b/a/0ba4e7a15cd397f4925b1b6fbb24a3efeca2567e_2_517x227.jpeg)

- 选`Yes`，填入相关信息

![image](https://cdn3.ldstatic.com/optimized/4X/3/3/d/33deabb969ecd97f9041f696662c759a746935b0_2_690x303.jpeg)

- 没有附加文件，继续`continue`

![iShot_2026-01-08_15.04.58](https://cdn3.ldstatic.com/optimized/4X/7/9/5/795a87dafe9e7cd5a2e4012a3caa9a3b1936b682_2_517x279.jpeg)

![iShot_2026-01-08_15.05.13](https://cdn3.ldstatic.com/optimized/4X/2/a/4/2a40d9ac20b875a11b48e5cd097ba443cb2c34c8_2_417x374.jpeg)
